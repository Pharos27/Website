Dawnyx Band

Shape your nights. Choose your mornings.

Overview Features Benefits Materials

The Dawnyx Band is a smartwatch that specifically improves waking up through a smarter, gentler alarm system. The device detects heart rate and orientation to tell you your heart rate in real time and to detect lighter sleep within a set wake-up window, allowing it to trigger the alarm at an optimal moment. Instead of loud sound, it uses a sequence of thermal stimulation on the wrist followed by escalating vibrations to wake the user gently without disturbing others. Built with custom firmware, dedicated sensors, and a purpose-designed enclosure, Dawnyx is optimised specifically for overnight use, prioritising comfort, calm awakenings, and waking on your body’s terms.





Features

Intuitive UI

The Dawnyx Band uses an energy-efficient resistive touchscreen which is satisfying to press and reduces accidental taps. Its simple, accessible UI is optimized for the small screen, enabling intentional navigation, full settings control, and watchface customization.

Progressive Wake-Up System

The Dawnyx Band uses multi-stage system to wake the user up as gently and effectively as possible. It begins with subtle warmth to stimulate the wrist, followed by vibrations that start softly and gradually increase in intensity to wake the user at the optimal moment.

Smart Wake Window

The Dawnyx Band uses a smart wake window to monitor heart rate and movement, detecting lighter stages of sleep within a set time range. This allows the alarm to trigger at the most natural moment, reducing sleep inertia and making wake-ups feel smoother, calmer, and far less abrupt.

Why Dawnyx?

Wake up without stress

Dawnyx replaces sudden alarms with a gradual, body-first wake-up that avoids panic and dazedness.

Sleep longer, wake smarter

Instead of forcing a fixed alarm time, Dawnyx waits for lighter sleep within your wake window to reduce grogginess.

No sound. No disruption.

Thermal and haptic cues wake only you. No noise, no guilt, no disturbing anyone else.
